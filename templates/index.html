This is the homepage for ScorchedSearch.com. To begin, search for a role with the search bar below and this site will search for and display relavant job listings of over multiple companys, such as General Motors, Amazon, Altair, and more, by either using the companies' APIs or by directly scraping the job listing data from the company pages. The goal of this project is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for job seekers to find relevant job listings across multiple companies without having to visit each company's website individually. By utilizing APIs and web scraping techniques, we aim to aggregate job listings in one place, making it easier for users to search and apply for jobs that match their skills and interests. scraping directly from the company pages. It also addresses the outdated nature of sites such as LinkedIn and Indeed by directly searching companies' job boards, so if a job isn't available it won't be found on the company's site.
